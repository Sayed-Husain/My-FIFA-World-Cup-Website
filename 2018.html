The 2018 FIFA World Cup

Background Information

The 2018 FIFA World Cup was the 21st FIFA World Cup, It took place in Russia from 14 June to 15 July 2018, after the country was awarded the hosting rights in 2010.

The 2018 FIFA World Cup held 64 matches in 12 venues across 11 cities, and it was the most engaging world cup (digatlly) to date According to Offical FIFA Reports.

According to Forbes, the FIFA World Cup 2018 was the most expensive world cup to date, with estimated costs of $14.2 billion.
